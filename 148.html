Containers vary over time. Condoms are by far the easiest and cheapest to use but I will spring for nicer containers from time to time. I would love to use the most secure containers all the time but it can get pricey. Below is a break down of the containers and their storage methods. I always store to the best of my abilities and ship in discrete packaging.

Condom  Easiest to have a lot of and obviously easiest to collect with. These are stored with the opening crimped shut and in a plastic baggie at room temperature.
Plastic Tube  These are usually used for collecting dog semen but work really well for storage and use. These are kept in a baggie in the refrigerator.
Glass Tube  Also kept refrigerated, these glass tubes keep the best. Glass doesn't affect flavor or color and the vial itself is thick enough to handle
Petri Dish  I really like these for storage as they stack and can keep a lot in a small space. The only down side is the lids can be removed more easily, but I secure them when shipping. Also refrigerated.
