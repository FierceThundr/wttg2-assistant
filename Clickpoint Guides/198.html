THE GREY DESCENDENTS

The arch was created by the Annunaki when the pyramids were first constructed and is the only functional piece of ancient alien technology left.

They left a portion of their kind here on earth to safeguard the technology.

These descendents have become who we now know as the grey aliens.

We will never see these beings as they live only on heavily protected military compounds in the US.

America offered them safety and luxurious living conditions as part of the agreement to transport the arch to America.
